F.A.Q

Will I get a tracking number for my shipment?
Yes! You will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number about 1-3 business days after your order is placed. Make sure you also check your SPAM folder just in case our email gets stuck there.

Will I get a tracking number for my shipment?

Items that are in stock and readily made will usually be shipped within 1-3 business days.

Do you ship worldwide?

We ship to all of America, Europe and some places in Asia.

How long will my order take to arrive?

Depending on your location, shipping within the US will take anywhere from 2-9 days after it has been shipped out.

How do I know what size to order?

We have a size chart under the t-shirts. Other than that most of our clothes are TRUE to size.

What is our return policy?

We only accept refunds for cases in which the item arrives damaged or if you have received the wrong item. If this is the case, please email us at organicstarboy@gmail.com and we will do our best to make the refund process as simple as possible. Final sale items cannot be returned.
