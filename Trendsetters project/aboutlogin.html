About Us


Trendsetter is a premier clothing brand that has become synonymous with style and fashion in Marilao, Bulacan. Founded in 2005 by a group of passionate fashion enthusiasts, Trendsetter set out to redefine the local fashion scene and provide trendy and high-quality clothing options to the community.

The founders, who were born and raised in Marilao, recognized the need for a fashion-forward clothing brand that could cater to the evolving tastes and preferences of the local residents. With a shared vision and a deep understanding of the market, they embarked on a journey to establish Trendsetter as the go-to destination for fashionable apparel. In the early days, Trendsetter started as a small boutique store in the heart of Marilao.

The founders carefully curated a collection of unique and trendy clothing pieces sourced from both local and international designers. Their commitment to offering the latest fashion trends, exceptional customer service, and a personalized shopping experience quickly gained the attention and loyalty of fashion-conscious individuals in the area. As the brand gained popularity, Trendsetter expanded its operations by opening additional stores in key locations across Marilao.

The success of the physical stores led to the launch of an online platform, allowing customers to conveniently browse and purchase Trendsetter's stylish clothing from the comfort of their homes. Over the years, Trendsetter has collaborated with local fashion influencers and designers to create exclusive collections that capture the essence of Marilao's vibrant fashion scene. These collaborations have not only elevated the brand's image but also empowered local talent and showcased their creativity to a wider audience. Today, Trendsetter stands as a trusted and iconic fashion brand in Marilao, known for its on-trend clothing, superior quality, and commitment to customer satisfaction. With a dedicated team of fashion experts, Trendsetter continues to evolve its collections, staying ahead of the fashion curve and inspiring individuals to express their unique style.
